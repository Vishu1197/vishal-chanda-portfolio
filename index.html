About Me

🔬 Vishal Chanda, PhD Researcher specializing in Bioinformatics, Nanotechnology, and Virology. My work integrates RNA-seq analytics, molecular simulations, and experimental virology to understand dengue virus pathogenesis and thrombocytopenia.

I design lipid-based nanocarriers, perform computational drug discovery, and validate results using in-vitro virology techniques.

My vision is to bridge AI-driven modeling with experimental biology to build next-generation antiviral therapeutics.
